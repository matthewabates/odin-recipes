Omelette

Description

Eggs are very versatile. This recipe uses ham and cheese to create a simple delicious omelette.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Ham
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. In a mixing bowl wisk the eggs
  2. Add a splash of milk
  3. In a hot pan melt some butter
  4. Pour wisked eggs into pan, moving contents around to cook evenly
  5. add ham and cheese as desired
  6. when done, fold the omelette and transfer to plate
