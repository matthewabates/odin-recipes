Hamburger Pie

Description

Very simple starter recipe, delicious results

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Cream of Celery
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Croisant Rolls

Steps

  1. Brown the meat
  2. In a baking dish combine and mix browned meat and cream of celery
  3. Add a layer of Shredded Cheese
  4. Top with Croisant Rolls
