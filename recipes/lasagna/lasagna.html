Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is very filling and meaty and delicious. here is a good recipe for lasagna

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Brown the meat
  2. in a baking dish start with a layer of lasagna noodles
  3. on top of the noodles add a layer of sauce, meat, and cheese
  4. continue layering, ending with cheese
  5. bake at 350 for 30 minutes, covered
  6. let rest for 20 minutes
